Tell Companies House you have verified someone’s identity

Use this service to tell us when you've verified someone's identity for Companies House.

You only need to verify someone's identity for Companies House once, unless we tell them they need to reverify.

What you'll need

You'll need to provide details about the:

  • person
  • identity checks you completed
  • documents the person provided

Tell us about the person

You'll need to tell us the person's details exactly as they appear on the documents you checked.

You'll also need to provide an email address for the person, which we'll use to send their Companies House personal code. The email address you give can only be used once to verify someone’s identity.

What details you need to provide about the person

You’ll need to tell us their:

  • full name
  • date of birth
  • home address
  • email address

Tell us about the identity checks

You must have completed checks that meet our identity verification standard. This lists which documents a person needs to provide and the checks you need to do to verify their identity.

What details you need to provide about the identity checks

You'll need to tell us:

  • if the documents were checked by a person, or using identity document validation technology (IDVT)
  • which documents the person provided
  • the date you completed the identity checks

Tell us about the person's documents

You do not need to upload copies of the documents you checked. However, for most documents, you'll need to provide some information from the document itself.

You must keep records of the identity checks you complete, including copies of the documents. We may ask to see these records.

What details you need to provide about the identity checks

For most documents, you'll need to provide some information from the document itself. You will not need to provide this information for any of the documents listed in 'group B' in the standard.

Where relevant, you'll need to provide the:

  • document reference – for example, you’ll need to give the passport number if you checked their passport
  • expiry date, if the document has one
  • country of issue, if the document has one
! Warning We could suspend or stop your business from acting as an authorised agent if you have not met our identity verification standard.

It takes around 10 minutes to use this service.

What we’ll show on the public register

When you verify someone’s identity, we'll ask you to confirm a statement to say you are satisfied the person is who they claim to be.

If the person connects their verified identity to their Companies House record as a director or person with significant control (PSC), we’ll show the statement on the public register to confirm they are verified.

Read the guidance about verifying someone's identity for Companies House to find out what information the statement will include.
