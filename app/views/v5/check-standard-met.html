Before you verify someone’s identity

To verify someone’s identity for Companies House, you must do checks that meet the identity verification standard.

You will need to make a declaration to confirm you you have met this standard. This declaration will be shown on the public register as part of any records about the person.

Read the guidance about verifying someone’s identity (opens in a new tab) to find out what you need to do to meet the verification standard.
