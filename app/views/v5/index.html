Routes to check a person’s identity

  • Are they a UK national?
  • Group 1: Primary identity documents
  • Group 2: Trusted government documents
  • Group 3: Financial and social history documents
  • Does at least one of the documents show their current address?
  • Is their address unusual?
  • Have you checked the address history from the past 5 years?
  • Documents meet GPG45 medium standard - NOT IN USE - user taken to keep-records page to continue
  • Documents do not meet GPG45 medium standard - Soft stop screen - user can still continue to the service
  • Do you have a birth certificate issued after the time of birth (UK, Isle of Man and Channel Islands)?
  • Does at least one of the documents show their current address and date of birth?
