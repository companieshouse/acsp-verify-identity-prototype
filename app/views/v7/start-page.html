Tell Companies House you have verified someone’s identity

Use this service to tell us about a person whose identity you have verified.

The checks you do to verify someone’s identity must meet Companies House identity verification standard. Read the guidance about what you need to do to meet the standard.

What you'll need

You’ll need to tell us what date you completed the identity checks and the person's:

  • full name
  • date of birth
  • home address
  • email address

Read the guidance about verifying someone's identity for Companies House.

It takes around 10 minutes to use this service.
