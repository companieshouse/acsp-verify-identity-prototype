Keep the number secure and do not share it with anyone outside of your organisation. You will need this to file on paper or using software.

What to do next

You can access your authorised agent account and services by signing in to your Companies House account.

If other people work for your business, you can add them to the authorised agent account.

Sign in to Companies House: https://find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk

Your responsibilities as an authorised agent

You must:

  • tell us if there is a change to the AML details you have provided – you must do this within 14 days
  • provide more information to Companies House if requested – this could be about your business, or records of your filings with Companies House
  • keep records of checks you do to verify someone’s identity for 7 years, if you verify the identity of clients for Companies House
