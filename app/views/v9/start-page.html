Tell Companies House you have verified someone’s identity

Use this service to tell us when you've verified someone's identity for Companies House.

You must have verified the person's identity by completing the checks from Companies House identity verification standard.

You'll need to keep records of the identity checks for 7 years from the date you completed them. We may ask to see these records to check you’ve met our standard.

! Warning We could suspend or stop your business from acting as an authorised agent if you have not met our identity verification standard.

What you'll need to tell us about the person

You’ll need to tell us their:

  • full name
  • date of birth
  • home address
  • email address

What you'll need to tell us about the identity checks

There are 2 options for checking identity documents in our identity verification standard. You’ll need to tell us:

  • whether you used option 1 or 2
  • which documents you checked
  • the date you completed the checks

It takes around 10 minutes to use this service.
