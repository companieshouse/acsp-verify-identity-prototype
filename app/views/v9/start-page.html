Tell Companies House you have verified someone’s identity

Use this service to tell us when you've verified someone's identity for Companies House.

What you'll need to tell us about the person

You’ll need to tell us their:

  • full name
  • date of birth
  • home address
  • email address

The email address you give can only be used once to verify someone’s identity. We'll use this to send their Companies House personal code.

What is a Companies House personal code?

This is an 11 character code that is given to a person once they’ve verified their identity.

It can be used to connect a person's verified identity to our records.

What you'll need to tell us about the identity checks

The identity checks you complete must meet our identity verification standard. The standard lists which documents can be used as evidence to verify a person’s identity.

You'll need to tell us:

  • if the documents were checked by a person, or using identity document validation technology (IDVT)
  • which documents were checked
  • the date the identity verification checks were completed

For relevant documents, you'll need to tell us the:

  • document reference – for example, for a passport you’ll need to give the passport number
  • expiry date, if the document has one
  • country of issue, if the document has one

You will not need to upload copies of the documents you checked. However, you will need to keep records of the identity checks, including copies of the documents. We may ask to see these records.

! Warning We could suspend or stop your business from acting as an authorised agent if you have not met our identity verification standard.

It takes around 10 minutes to use this service.
