Tell Companies House you have verified someone’s identity

Use this service to tell us about a person whose identity you have verified.

What you'll need

You’ll need the person’s:

  • professional or honorary title (if relevant)
  • full name
  • date of birth
  • home address
  • email address

You’ll also need to tell us which documents you checked to verify their identity and the date you completed the checks.
