Tell Companies House you have verified someone’s identity

Use this service to tell us about a person whose identity you have verified.

What you'll need

You’ll need to have copies of the documents you used to check the person’s identity. You must enter the person's details exactly as they are shown on the documents.

You’ll need to tell us their:

  • full name
  • date of birth
  • home address

You’ll also need to tell us :

  • which documents you checked to verify their identity
  • the date you completed the identity checks
  • the person’s email address
