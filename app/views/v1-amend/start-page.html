Use this service to tell us when you want to change details regarding someone you identity verified for Companies House.

What information you will need to provide

You’ll need the following information to amend their record:

  • Identity Verification Reference Number
  • Email address
  • Date of Birth (if you intend on amending the email address)
  • CH Personal code?
What is a Companies House personal code?

This is an 11 character code that is given to a person once they’ve verified their identity.

It can be used to connect a person's verified identity to our records.
